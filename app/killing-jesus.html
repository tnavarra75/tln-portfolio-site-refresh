Killing Jesus Website

National Geographic Channel

retouching, production design

photoshop

Each scene in this parallax website was supplied as a layered PSD from the illustrator. I was responsible for isolating the layers into individual files and cleaning up the edges — cloning in any areas that were incomplete once isolated. In motion it was often discovered there was a need to extend or manipulate the illustration to work in relation with other pieces. I worked closely with the motion designer to provide assets as needed.
